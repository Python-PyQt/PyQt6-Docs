A viewport on the Qt3D Scene.

:sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DRender.QViewport` of the scene specifies at which portion of the render surface Qt3D is rendering to. Area outside the viewport is left untouched. It also controls global parameters to the rendering in that viewport like gamma.
