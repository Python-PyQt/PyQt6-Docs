Sets whether the message will be replied later (if *enable* is true) or if an automatic reply should be generated by Qt D-Bus (if *enable* is false).

In D-Bus, all method calls must generate a reply to the caller, unless the caller explicitly indicates otherwise (see :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDBus.QDBusMessage.isReplyRequired`). `QtDBus <https://doc.qt.io/qt-6/qtdbus-index.html>`_ automatically generates such replies for any slots being called, but it also allows slots to indicate whether they will take responsibility of sending the reply at a later time, after the function has finished processing.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDBus.QDBusMessage.isDelayedReply`, `Delayed Replies <https://doc.qt.io/qt-6/qdbusdeclaringslots.html#delayed-replies>`_.
