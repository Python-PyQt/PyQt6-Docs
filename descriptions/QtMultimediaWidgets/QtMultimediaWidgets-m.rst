.. sip:module-description::
    :status:    done
    :brief:     Provides additional multimedia related widgets and controls

The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtMultimediaWidgets` module contains classes providing
additional multimedia-related widgets and controls.  The classes expand the
capabilities of the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtMultimedia` and
:sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets` modules.
