A class holding information about `Source <https://doc.qt.io/qt-6/qtremoteobjects-source.html#source>`_ objects available on the Qt Remote Objects network.

The Registry is a special Source/Replica pair held by a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtRemoteObjects.QRemoteObjectNode` itself. It knows about all other `Source <https://doc.qt.io/qt-6/qtremoteobjects-source.html#source>`_s available on the network, and simplifies the process of connecting to other :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtRemoteObjects.QRemoteObjectNode`\ s.
