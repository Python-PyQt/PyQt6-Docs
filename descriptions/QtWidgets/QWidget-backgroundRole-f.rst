Returns the background role of the widget.

The background role defines the brush from the widget's :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.palette` that is used to render the background.

If no explicit background role is set, the widget inherits its parent widget's background role.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setBackgroundRole`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.foregroundRole`.
