Constructs a scroll bar with the given *orientation*.

The *parent* argument is passed to the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget` constructor.

The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QAbstractSlider.minimum` defaults to 0, the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QAbstractSlider.maximum` to 99, with a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QAbstractSlider.singleStep` size of 1 and a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QAbstractSlider.pageStep` size of 10, and an initial :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QAbstractSlider.value` of 0.
