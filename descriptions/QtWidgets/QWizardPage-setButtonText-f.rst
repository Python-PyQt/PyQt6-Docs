Sets the text on button *which* to be *text* on this page.

By default, the text on buttons depends on the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWizard.wizardStyle`, but may be redefined for the wizard as a whole using :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWizard.setButtonText`.
