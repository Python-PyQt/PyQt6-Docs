This enum is used to hold information about the type of the style option, and is defined for each :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyleOption` subclass.

The type is used internally by :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyleOption`, its subclasses, and qstyleoption_cast() to determine the type of style option. In general you do not need to worry about this unless you want to create your own :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyleOption` subclass and your own styles.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyleOptionTab.StyleOptionVersion.StyleOptionVersion`.
