Sets the span angle for an ellipse segment to *angle*, which is in 16ths of a degree. This angle is used together with :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QGraphicsEllipseItem.startAngle` to represent an ellipse segment (a pie). By default, the span angle is 5760 (360 \* 16, a full ellipse).
