This enum defines two constants that can be used to extract the two components of :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QFrame.frameStyle`:

Normally, you don't need to use these, since :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QFrame.frameShadow` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QFrame.frameShape` already extract the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QFrame.Shadow.Shadow` and the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QFrame.Shape.Shape` parts of :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QFrame.frameStyle`.
