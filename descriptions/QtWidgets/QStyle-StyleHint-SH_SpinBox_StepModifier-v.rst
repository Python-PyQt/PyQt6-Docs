Determines which :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.KeyboardModifiers` increases the step rate of :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QAbstractSpinBox`. Possible values are :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.KeyboardModifiers.NoModifier`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.KeyboardModifiers.ControlModifier` (default) or :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.KeyboardModifiers.ShiftModifier`. :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.KeyboardModifiers.NoModifier` disables this feature. This enum value has been introduced in Qt 5.12.
