Hides the widget. This function is equivalent to :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setVisible`\ (false).

**Note:** If you are working with :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QDialog` or its subclasses and you invoke the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.show` function after this function, the dialog will be displayed in its original position.
