Shows the widget and its child widgets.

For child windows, this is equivalent to calling :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setVisible`\ (true). Otherwise, it is equivalent to calling :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.showFullScreen`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.showMaximized`, or :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setVisible`\ (true), depending on the platform's default behavior for the window flags.
