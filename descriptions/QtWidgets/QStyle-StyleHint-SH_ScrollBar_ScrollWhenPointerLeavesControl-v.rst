A boolean value. If true, when clicking a scroll bar :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle.SubControls.SubControl`, holding the mouse button down and moving the pointer outside the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle.SubControls.SubControl`, the scroll bar continues to scroll. If false, the scollbar stops scrolling when the pointer leaves the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle.SubControls.SubControl`.
