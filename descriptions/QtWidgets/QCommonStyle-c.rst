The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QCommonStyle` class encapsulates the common Look and Feel of a GUI.

This abstract class implements some of the widget's look and feel that is common to all GUI styles provided and shipped as part of Qt.

Since :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QCommonStyle` inherits :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle`, all of its functions are fully documented in the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle` documentation.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QProxyStyle`.
