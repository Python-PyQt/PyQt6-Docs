A boolean value. If true, when clicking a scroll bar button (\ :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle.SubControls.SC_ScrollBarAddLine` or :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QStyle.SubControls.SC_ScrollBarSubLine`) and dragging over to the opposite button (rolling) will press the new button and release the old one. When it is false, the original button is released and nothing happens (like a push button).
