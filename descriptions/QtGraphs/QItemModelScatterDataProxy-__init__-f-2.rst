Constructs :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGraphs.QItemModelScatterDataProxy` with *itemModel* and an optional *parent*. The proxy doesn't take ownership of the *itemModel*, as item models are typically owned by other controls. The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGraphs.QItemModelScatterDataProxy.xPosRole` property is set to *xPosRole*, the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGraphs.QItemModelScatterDataProxy.yPosRole` property to *yPosRole*, and the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGraphs.QItemModelScatterDataProxy.zPosRole` property to *zPosRole*.
