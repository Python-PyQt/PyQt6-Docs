The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DExtras.QSphereGeometry` class allows creation of a sphere in 3D space.

The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DExtras.QSphereGeometry` class is most commonly used internally by the QSphereMesh but can also be used in custom :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DRender.QGeometryRenderer` subclasses.
