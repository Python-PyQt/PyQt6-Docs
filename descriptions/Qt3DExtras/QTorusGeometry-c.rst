The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DExtras.QTorusGeometry` class allows creation of a torus in 3D space.

The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DExtras.QTorusGeometry` class is most commonly used internally by the QTorusMesh but can also be used in custom :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DRender.QGeometryRenderer` subclasses.
