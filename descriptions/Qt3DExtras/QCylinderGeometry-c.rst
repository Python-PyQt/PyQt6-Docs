The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DExtras.QCylinderGeometry` class allows creation of a cylinder in 3D space.

The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DExtras.QCylinderGeometry` class is most commonly used internally by the QCylinderMesh but can also be used in custom :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.Qt3DRender.QGeometryRenderer` subclasses.
