The key codes are listed in :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.Key` and can be combined with modifiers (see :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.Modifiers`) such as :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.Modifiers.SHIFT`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.Modifiers.CTRL`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.Modifiers.ALT`, or :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.Modifiers.META`.
