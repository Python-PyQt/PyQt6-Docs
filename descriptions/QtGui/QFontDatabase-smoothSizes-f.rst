Returns the point sizes of a font that has family *family* and style *styleName* that will look attractive. The list may be empty. For non-scalable fonts and bitmap scalable fonts, this function is equivalent to :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QFontDatabase.pointSizes`.
