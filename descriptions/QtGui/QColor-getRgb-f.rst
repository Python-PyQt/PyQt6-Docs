Sets the contents pointed to by *r*, *g*, *b*, and *a*, to the red, green, blue, and alpha-channel (transparency) components of the color's RGB value.

These components can be retrieved individually using the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.red`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.green`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.blue` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.alpha` functions.
