Sets the *width* used for text list and text block indenting.

The indent properties of :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QTextListFormat` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QTextBlockFormat` specify multiples of this value. The default indent width is 40 .
