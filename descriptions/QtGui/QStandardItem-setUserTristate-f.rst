Sets whether the item is tristate and controlled by the user. If *tristate* is true, the user can cycle through three separate states; otherwise, the item is checkable with two states. (Note that this also requires that the item is checkable; see :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QStandardItem.isCheckable`.)
