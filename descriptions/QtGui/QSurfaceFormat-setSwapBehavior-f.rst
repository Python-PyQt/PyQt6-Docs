Set the swap *behavior* of the surface.

The swap behavior specifies whether single, double, or triple buffering is desired. The default, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSurfaceFormat.SwapBehavior.DefaultSwapBehavior`, gives the default swap behavior of the platform.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSurfaceFormat.swapBehavior`.
