Constructs a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSyntaxHighlighter` with the given *parent*.

If the parent is a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QTextEdit`, it installs the syntax highlighter on the parents document. The specified :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QTextEdit` also becomes the owner of the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSyntaxHighlighter`.
