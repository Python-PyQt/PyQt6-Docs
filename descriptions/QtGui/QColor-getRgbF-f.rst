Sets the contents pointed to by *r*, *g*, *b*, and *a*, to the red, green, blue, and alpha-channel (transparency) components of the color's RGB value.

These components can be retrieved individually using the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.redF`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.greenF`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.blueF` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QColor.alphaF` functions.
