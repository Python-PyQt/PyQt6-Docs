The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSurface` class is an abstraction of renderable surfaces in Qt.

The size of the surface is accessible with the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSurface.size` function. The rendering specific attributes of the surface are accessible through the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QSurface.format` function.
