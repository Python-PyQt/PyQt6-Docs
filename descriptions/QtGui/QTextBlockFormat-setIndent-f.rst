Sets the paragraph's *indentation*. Margins are set independently of indentation with :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QTextBlockFormat.setLeftMargin` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QTextBlockFormat.setTextIndent`. The *indentation* is an integer that is multiplied with the document-wide standard indent, resulting in the actual indent of the paragraph.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QTextBlockFormat.indent`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QTextDocument.indentWidth`.
