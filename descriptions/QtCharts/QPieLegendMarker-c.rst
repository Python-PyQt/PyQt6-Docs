The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCharts.QPieLegendMarker` class is a legend marker for a pie series.

A pie legend marker is related to :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCharts.QPieSeries`. With a pie series, each slice of the pie is related to one marker in the legend.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCharts.QLegend`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCharts.QPieSeries`, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCharts.QPieSlice`.
