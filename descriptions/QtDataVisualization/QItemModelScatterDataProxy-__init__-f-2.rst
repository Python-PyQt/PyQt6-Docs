Constructs :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.QItemModelScatterDataProxy` with *itemModel* and optional *parent*. Proxy doesn't take ownership of the *itemModel*, as typically item models are owned by other controls. The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.QItemModelScatterDataProxy.xPosRole` property is set to *xPosRole*, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.QItemModelScatterDataProxy.yPosRole` property to *yPosRole*, and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.QItemModelScatterDataProxy.zPosRole` property to *zPosRole*.
