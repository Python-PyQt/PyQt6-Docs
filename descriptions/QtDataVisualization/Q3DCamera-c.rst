Representation of a camera in 3D space.

:sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.Q3DCamera` represents a basic orbit around centerpoint 3D camera that is used when rendering the data visualization. The class offers simple methods for rotating the camera around the origin and setting zoom level.
