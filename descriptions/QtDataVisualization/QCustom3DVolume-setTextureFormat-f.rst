Sets the format of the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.QCustom3DVolume.textureData` property to *format*. Only two formats are supported currently: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QImage.Format.Format_Indexed8` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QImage.Format.Format_ARGB32`. If an indexed format is specified, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtDataVisualization.QCustom3DVolume.colorTable` must also be set. Defaults to :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QImage.Format.Format_ARGB32`.
