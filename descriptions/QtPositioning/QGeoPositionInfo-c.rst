The :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtPositioning.QGeoPositionInfo` class contains information gathered on a global position, direction and velocity at a particular point in time.

A :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtPositioning.QGeoPositionInfo` contains, at a minimum, a geographical coordinate and a timestamp. It may also have heading and speed measurements as well as estimates of the accuracy of the provided data.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtPositioning.QGeoPositionInfoSource`.
