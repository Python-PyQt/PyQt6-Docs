The accuracy of the provided bearing, in degrees. This attribute is available only on Android (API level 26 or above) and macOS/iOS. See corresponding `Android <https://doc.qt.io/qt-6/https://developer.android.com/reference/android/location/Location.html#getBearingAccuracyDegrees%28%29>`_ and `Apple <https://doc.qt.io/qt-6/https://developer.apple.com/documentation/corelocation/cllocation/3524338-courseaccuracy?language=objc>`_ documentation for more details.
