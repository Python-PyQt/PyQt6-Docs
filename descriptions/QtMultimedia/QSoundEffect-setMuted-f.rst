Sets whether to mute this sound effect's playback.

If *muted* is true, playback will be muted (silenced), and otherwise playback will occur with the currently specified :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtMultimedia.QSoundEffect.volume`.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtMultimedia.QSoundEffect.isMuted`.
