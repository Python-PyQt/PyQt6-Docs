The reader reports characters in :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QXmlStreamReader.text`. If the characters are all white-space, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QXmlStreamReader.isWhitespace` returns ``true``. If the characters stem from a CDATA section, :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QXmlStreamReader.isCDATA` returns ``true``.
