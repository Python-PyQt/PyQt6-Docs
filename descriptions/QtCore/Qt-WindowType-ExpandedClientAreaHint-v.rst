Requests that the window's client area is expanded to areas where it might be obscured by, or conflicting with other UI elements, such as the window's titlebar controls or other system UIs. The window's :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtGui.QWindow.safeAreaMargins` will reflect any areas that may have conflicting UI elements.
