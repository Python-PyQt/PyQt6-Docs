Constructs a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QRectF` rectangle from the given :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QRect` *rectangle*.

**Note:** This function, like :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QRect.toRectF`, preserves the :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QRectF.size` of *rectangle*, not its :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QRectF.bottomRight` corner.
