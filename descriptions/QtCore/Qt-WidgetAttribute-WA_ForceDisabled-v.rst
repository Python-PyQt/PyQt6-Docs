Indicates that the widget is explicitly disabled, i.e. it will remain disabled even when all its ancestors are set to the enabled state. This implies WA_Disabled. This is set/cleared by :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setEnabled` and :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setDisabled`.
