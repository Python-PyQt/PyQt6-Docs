Indicates that updates are explicitly disabled for the widget; i.e. it will remain disabled even when all its ancestors are set to the updates-enabled state. This implies WA_UpdatesDisabled. This is set/cleared by :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWidgets.QWidget.setUpdatesEnabled`.
