Sets the port of the URL to *port*. The port is part of the authority of the URL, as described in :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QUrl.setAuthority`.

*port* must be between 0 and 65535 inclusive. Setting the port to -1 indicates that the port is unspecified.

.. seealso:: :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QUrl.port`.
