The type of the simplest lightweight time representations.

This enumeration identifies a type of lightweight time representation to pass to a :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.QTimeZone` constructor, where no further data are required. They correspond to the like-named members of :sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtCore.Qt.TimeSpec`.
