This enum specifies where the ellipsis should appear when displaying texts that don't fit:

Qt::ElideMiddle is normally the most appropriate choice for URLs (e.g., "`http://bugreports.qt.../QTWEBSITE-13/ <http://bugreports.qt.io/browse/QTWEBSITE-13>`_"), whereas Qt::ElideRight is appropriate for other strings (e.g., "`Deploying Applications on Ma... <http://doc.qt.io/archives/qq/qq09-mac-deployment.html>`_").
