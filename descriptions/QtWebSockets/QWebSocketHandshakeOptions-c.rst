Collects options for the WebSocket handshake.

:sip:ref:`~PyQt6.QtWebSockets.QWebSocketHandshakeOptions` collects options that are passed along to the WebSocket handshake, such as WebSocket subprotocols and WebSocket Extensions.

At the moment, only WebSocket subprotocols are supported.
